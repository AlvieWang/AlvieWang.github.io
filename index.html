About Me 👋

I am a first-year MPhil student at CUHK(Shenzhen), advised by Prof. Zhizheng Wu in the School of Data Science and AI Institute.

My research centers on building intelligent agents that can understand and act in the physical world. Specifically:

  • 🔗 Developing Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models for embodied task planning
  • ⚖️ Designing preference alignment methods (DPO) for safer robot behavior
  • 🏗️ Creating evaluation benchmarks with multi-dimensional metrics
  • 🌉 Bridging the sim-to-real gap via domain adaptation

Previously, I earned my B.E. from Guangdong University of Technology and interned at the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences on DeepSeek-OCR.

Staged VLA Framework for Embodied Task Planning

A novel staged fine-tuning framework that adapts general-purpose VLMs into effective embodied task planners through progressive skill acquisition. Three-stage curriculum learning reduces catastrophic forgetting while Bias-DPO handles long-tail physical constraint violations.
